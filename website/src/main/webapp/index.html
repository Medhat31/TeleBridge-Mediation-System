Why Choose TeleBridge?

Built for the scale and reliability demands of modern telecommunications networks.

Intelligent Filtering

Dynamically drop zero-duration and emergency calls (e.g., 911, 112) based on highly configurable Mediation Rules before they ever reach billing systems.

Secure FTP/SFTP Transfer

Securely connect to upstream and downstream nodes utilizing industry-standard File Transfer Protocols with robust error handling and retries.

Automated Deduplication

Prevent duplicate billing cycles by utilizing MD5-hashed CDR fingerprinting. Duplicate records are automatically purged at the engine level.

Node Orchestration

Easily orchestrate multiple network elements, from Mobile Switching Centers (MSC) and Packet Data Networks (PGW), out to external Fraud and Charging nodes.
